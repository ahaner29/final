Climate Change

Climate change is real ; it's a proven fact! The Earth's climate continues to change throughout history. The current warming trend is important since it is the result of everything
that's happened since the mid-20th century and continues at a rapid rate. But as individuals we have the power to slow down climate change by enacting little changes within our community.

Ten Ways You Can Help Stop Climate Change

  1. Green your commute . Transportation causes about 24% of climate-polluting emissions. Reducing your transportation will not only help against climate change, it can
    save you a few dollars as well. A few things you can do instead are taking public transit, ride a bike, carpool, fly less, etc.

  2. Use energy wisely . By being more energy efficient, you will pollute less, and again, save money. A few things you can do: change to energy-efficient light bulbs,wash
    your clothes in cold or warm water, unplug electronics when not in use, hang dry clothes when you can instead of a dryer, etc.

  3. Change your diet . The following are four simple changes to your diet that will reduce it's climate impact: eat meat-free meals, buy organic or local food instead, don't
    waste food, and start your own garden. Just a FYI: eating insects works, too!

  4. Divest from fossil fuel . Spread the word to your friends, family, workplace, pension fund, and university to stop funding fossil fuels to let them know you care about
    climate change.

  5. Spread awareness . People are more influenced by their friends and families than by experts, so talk to your friends and family about global warming. It's important to
    everyone on board and help make an impact.

  6. Say no to plastic . Things like taking a reusable or cloth bag when shopping instead of plastic bags and avoiding products with lots of packaging can help slow down
    climate change.

  7. Reuse and recycle . When you recycle and reuse products, like paper and bottles, it has the potential to help protect the environment. *Use recycled paper and recycle
    your office and household waste!

  8. Green power . Switch to electricity that uses energy sources with low (or no) carbon dioxide emissions. An example of this is solar panels; it uses the sun's rays as a
    source of energy for generating electricity and heat.

  9. Act against forest lost . There's tons you can do with this! Plant a tree, avoid anything that may be a fire hazard, and buy wood with a seal that shows it has a sustain-
    able origin are all things you can do to protect forests!

  10. Use your voice ! Demand that the government take measures to fight against climate change in any way they can. People forget they have an impact on the government
    and they can use their voices to demand the government to raise awareness about global warming.






    11. Everything little thing matters! I challenge you to make a difference and enact change in your society.
